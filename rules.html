Void Hunters Game Rules

  • Objective: Defeat enemies, collect upgrades, and survive as long as possible to increase your score.
  • Health: The player's health is displayed as a red bar. If it reaches 0, the game ends.
  • Experience: Defeat enemies to gain experience (EXP). Level up to unlock more upgrades.
  • Upgrades: Choose from a variety of upgrades to increase damage, fire rate, movement speed, and more. These upgrades help you survive longer.
  • Turbo Mode: Charge your turbo power and activate it to double your stats temporarily.
  • Score: Your score is displayed in the top-right corner. It increases with each enemy defeated.
  • Controls: Use the mouse to move and shoot automatically.
  • Game Over: The game ends when your health reaches 0. Try to achieve the highest score!
